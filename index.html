Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service available outside Moradabad? +
We're currently serving Moradabad and actively expanding to nearby cities including Rampur, Amroha, and Sambhal. Register your interest and we'll notify you when we launch in your area.
What happens after I register my pet? +
Our care coordinator will reach out on WhatsApp within 24 hours to confirm your enrollment, review your pet's health details, and share your customized preventive care calendar.
Is registration paid? +
No — registration is completely free. After you register, our care coordinator will WhatsApp you within 24 hours to complete your pet's profile and explain how our care plans work. No pressure, no hidden charges — you decide what's right for your pet.
How is my pet's data kept private? +
We never share your personal or pet data with third parties outside our clinic network. All data is stored securely and used only to provide your pet's care services. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
Can I register multiple pets? +
Absolutely! Simply submit a separate registration form for each pet. Our team will create individual health profiles and care plans for each of your furry family members.
